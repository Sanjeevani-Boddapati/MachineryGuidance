INFORMATION OF TRACTOR MOUNTED SPRAYERS

A sprayer is a machine used to spray any kind of liquid, sprayers are most commonly used for projection of water, weed killers, crop performance materials, pest maintenance chemicals, as well as manufacturing and production line ingredients. In agriculture, a sprayer t is used to apply herbicides, pesticides, and fertilizers on agricultural crops. A Tractor-mounted sprayer is one which is fixed on to a tractor. The capacity of the tank ranges from 150 to 500 gallons. A hydraulic motor attached to the power take-off shaft drives the pump of the sprayer. Its special feature is its Sturdiness. It requires minimal maintenance and can be used life long.
