INFORMATION OF MOULD BOARD PLOUGH

It is tractor operated implement and it consists of share point, share, mouldboard, landside, and frog, shank, frame and hitch system. The share point is of bar type and is made from high carbon steel or low alloy steel. The share is also made from high carbon steel or low alloy steel. Both are hardened and tempered to suitable hardness (about 45 ARC). The working of the plough is controlled by hydraulic system of the tractor. Mould board plough is a primary tillage implement.
