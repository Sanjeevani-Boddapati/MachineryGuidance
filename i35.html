INFORMATION OF DISC PLOUGH

A disc plough works in all types of soil to break, turn and mix the soil. It is majorly used to prepare the new fields for farming and process the stony areas. Makes ploughing easier in rocky and rooted areas. Very useful in hard and dry trashy land conditions. Disc ploughs are of two types' viz., Standard disc plough and Vertical disc plough. It consists of steel disc of 60 to 90 cm diameter, set at a certain angle to the direction of travel.
